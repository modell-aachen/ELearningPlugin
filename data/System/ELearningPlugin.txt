---++ List tours

The following section lists tours available for the current topic. You can include it from a suitable place in your template(s).

%STARTSECTION{name="tours"}%%SOLRSEARCH{
  "form:*ELearningTourForm field_StartTopic_s:(%IF{"defined el_tourid" then="%URLPARAM{"el_tourid"}%" else="%BASETOPIC% OR %BASEWEB%.%BASETOPIC%"}%)"
  fields="webtopic"
  format="$percntQWIKITOUR{\"$webtopic\" step=\"%URLPARAM{"el_step" default=""}%\"}$percnt"
}%%ENDSECTION{name="tours"}%

Alternatively, the following section contains the same information wrapped in a twisty element.

%STARTSECTION{name="tours_twisty"}%%SOLRSEARCH{
  "form:*ELearningTourForm field_StartTopic_s:(%IF{"defined el_tourid" then="%URLPARAM{"el_tourid"}%" else="%BASETOPIC% OR %BASEWEB%.%BASETOPIC%"}%)"
  fields="webtopic"
  header="$percntTWISTY{id=\"elearningToursTwisty\" firststart=\"%display{default="hide"}%\" remember=\"on\" noscript=\"hide\" showlink=\"%MAKETEXT{"Guided tours"}%\" hidelink=\"%MAKETEXT{"Guided tours"}%\" showimgleft=\"%ICONURLPATH{arrow-right}%\" hideimgleft=\"%ICONURLPATH{arrow-down}%\" linkclass=\"patternTwistyButton\"}$percnt"
  format="$percntQWIKITOUR{\"$webtopic\" step=\"%URLPARAM{"el_step" default=""}%\"}$percnt"
  footer="$percntENDTWISTY$percnt"
}%%ENDSECTION{name="tours_twisty"}%
